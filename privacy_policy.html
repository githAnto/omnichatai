OmniChatAI - Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 1, 2025

1. Overview

OmniChatAI ("we", "us", or "our") operates the OmniChatAI mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

2. Information Collection and Use

OmniChatAI does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Our App is a simple launcher that provides quick access links to third-party AI services. We do not:

  • Collect personal data
  • Track user behavior
  • Store browsing history
  • Access device information beyond what's required for the app to function
  • Use analytics or advertising services
  • Require user accounts or registration

3. Third-Party Services

OmniChatAI provides convenient access to the following third-party AI services:

  • ChatGPT (OpenAI)
  • Claude (Anthropic)
  • Gemini (Google)
  • Perplexity
  • Phind
  • You.com
  • Grok (X)
  • HuggingChat (Hugging Face)

Each of these services has its own privacy policy and terms of service. When you use these services through our App, you are subject to their respective privacy policies. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of each service:

  • OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage

OmniChatAI does not store any user data on our servers. The App does not use cloud storage, databases, or any backend services. All functionality is contained within the app itself.

5. Permissions

The App requires internet access to connect to third-party AI services. We do not request any other device permissions.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age. However, the third-party AI services accessed through our App may have age restrictions. Please review their respective policies.

7. Security

Since we do not collect or store any user data, there is no user data to secure. Your interactions with third-party AI services are governed by their respective security policies.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

9. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. If you have concerns about data collected by the third-party AI services, please contact them directly.
